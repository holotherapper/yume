Shibuya, one of Tokyo's busiest districts, is undergoing a massive urban transformation. New high-rise developments like Scramble Square and Sakura Stage have reshaped the skyline, while long-standing shops along Dogenzaka and Center-gai face rising rents. The population is a mix of office workers, shop owners, students, freelancers, and retirees. Each person navigates daily life with their own goals, relationships, and pressures in this dense, fast-changing neighborhood.
